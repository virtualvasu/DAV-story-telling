Scattered Spending

To make my visualization even more insightful, I created a scatter plot where each dot represented a transaction, and its size was proportional to the amount spent.

Takeaways:

  • It reinforced the idea that while smaller transactions are frequent, the larger ones make up a significant portion of total spending.

Do Weekends Cost Me More?

To wrap up, I looked at how much I spent on each weekday using a bar chart.

Findings:

  • Sunday saw the highest spending followed by Monday, likely due to I stock all my snacks for the week.
  • Midweek payments were relatively lower, with most transactions happening on Monday and Sunday‚Äîprobably routine payments like groceries or online orders.
  • Slight spending peak on Thursday, with most transactions happening on Monday and Sunday‚Äîprobably due to snacks running out, I spend again to refil them.
